Flavour¶

Some widgets allow further configuration with the option “flavour”

The following flavor names are defined:

inside Design “metal”¶

  • black
  • white <---hite :)
  • antimony
  • boron
  • lithium
  • potassium
  • sodium

inside Design “pure”¶

  • black
  • white <---white :)

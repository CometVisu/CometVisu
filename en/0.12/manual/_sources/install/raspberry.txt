Installation on Raspberry PI
============================

The CometVisu needs relatively few resources and software
packages and therefore runs very well on a
`Raspberry Pi <http://de.wikipedia.org/wiki/Raspberry_Pi>`__

A quick and easy way to get a Raspberry Pi running as a
"CometVisu server" is to use the `KNX User Forum bereitgestellten
Images <http://knx-user-forum.de/knx-eib-forum/24860-raspberry-image-eibd-linknx-mit-cometvisu.html>`__

To bring the CometVisu included in the image up to date
you simply follow the :doc:`Update-Anweisungen <update-other>`

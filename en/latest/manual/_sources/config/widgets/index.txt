.. rubric:: Widgets in the CometVisu

Widgets are the individual elements from which a CometVisu ​​site
Is constructed. These can either be fixed in the CometVisu
Can be installed or integrated via a plug-in interface. The
Widgets can be added by the editor. (Right click on the
Element to receive a widget. A page or a
Group (group). By selecting the widget from the drop-down menu and
Then the widget is inserted and clicked on the + sign
Can be configured as follows;


Operatable widgets
==================

These widgets can be operated in any way to send data to the backend or trigger other
actions, e.g. you can switch on a lamp by clicking a switch-widget, or you can navigate withon the visu with a Pagejump.

+-------------------------------------------------+-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| Widget                                          | Description                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               |
+=================================================+===========================================================================================================================================================================================================================================================================================================+
| :doc:`Audio <audio/index>`                      | With this widget, an audio file can be integrated into the Visu, which can be played by a GA.                                                                                                                                                                                                             |
+-------------------------------------------------+-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| :doc:`Designtoggle <designtoggle/index>`        | Adds a button to the Visu that allows you to change the design.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           |
+-------------------------------------------------+-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| :doc:`ImageTrigger <imagetrigger/index>`        | Adds a clickable symbol to the Visu. This may be e.g. For reminders such as birthdays or garbage disposal.                                                                                                                                                                                                |
+-------------------------------------------------+-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| :doc:`InfoAction <infoaction/index>`            | Enables the combination of an illustrative and usable widgets.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            |
+-------------------------------------------------+-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| :doc:`InfoTrigger <infotrigger/index>`          | Adds an element to the Visu that contains two buttons and a value indication for feedback from the BUS. (E.g. For multimedial control.                                                                                                                                                                    |
+-------------------------------------------------+-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| :doc:`Multitrigger <multitrigger/index>`        | Adds a widget with 4 buttons to the Visu. Thus, e.g. Change the operating mode of the heating system (Comfort -> Night -> Absent -> Frost protection) or create scene functions.                                                                                                                          |
+-------------------------------------------------+-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| :doc:`Pagejump <pagejump/index>`                | The pagejump widget can be used to link the Visu pages. The function is similar to hyperlinks.                                                                                                                                                                                                            |
+-------------------------------------------------+-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| :doc:`PushButton <pushbutton/index>`            | Adds a button to the Visu that sends a defined value to the BUS when you press and release. E.g. Pushing a 1 and releasing a 0. This makes it possible, for example, to simulate a push button to open and close a garage door, blinds or blinds.                                                         |
+-------------------------------------------------+-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| :doc:`Refresh <refresh/index>`                  | With the widget refresh, the Visu is added a switch, which allows the Visu to be reloaded in the browser?                                                                                                                                                                                                 |
+-------------------------------------------------+-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| :doc:`Reload <reload/index>`                    | With the reload widget, the Visu is added a switch, which allows to reload the Visu in the browser?                                                                                                                                                                                                       |
+-------------------------------------------------+-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| :doc:`Slide <slide/index>`                      | Adds a horizontal slider to the visu. This can be used, for example, Dimming or temperature values.                                                                                                                                                                                                       |
+-------------------------------------------------+-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| :doc:`Switch <switch/index>`                    | Adds a button to the Visu. Switching actions can be triggered. The switch is typically used for ON/OFF actions. (E.g., light, sockets, etc.)                                                                                                                                                              |
+-------------------------------------------------+-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| :doc:`Toggle <toggle/index>`                    | The toggle widget is similar to the switch, but it can take more than two states. Can be used to change the operating modes of the heating system. Each time the toggle is pressed, the toggle takes the next possible state.                                                                             |
+-------------------------------------------------+-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| :doc:`Trigger <trigger/index>`                  | Adds a button to the visu with which exactly a defined value for a short, as well as a defined value for a long key pressure, can be sent to the BUS, e.g. For recalling and storing scenes or driving roller blinds. (Short = stop, long = drive). The GA for short and long term may vary.              |
+-------------------------------------------------+-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| :doc:`URL-trigger <urltrigger/index>`           | The UrlTrigger widget adds a button to the visualization that queries data from a URL in the background.                                                                                                                                                                                                  |
+-------------------------------------------------+-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+

Informative widgets
===================

These widgets have a purely informative function, an operation is not possible.

+---------------------------------------------------+---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| Widget                                            | Description                                                                                                                           |
+===================================================+=======================================================================================================================================+
| :doc:`Image <image/index>`                        | Adds a static image or a camera image to the visu.                                                                                    |
+---------------------------------------------------+---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| :doc:`Info <info/index>`                          | Adds a dynamic field to the Visu that can represent values from the BUS. (e.g. 14-byte text or counter headers)                       |
+---------------------------------------------------+---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| :doc:`Line <line/index>`                          | Adds a horizontal line to the visu to divide the visual side into individual areas.                                                   |
+---------------------------------------------------+---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| :doc:`RGB <rgb/index>`                            | With the RGB widget you can display a color in the Visu.                                                                              |
+---------------------------------------------------+---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| :doc:`Text <text/index>`                          | Adds a static text field to the Visu.                                                                                                 |
+---------------------------------------------------+---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| :doc:`Video <video/index>`                        | Adds a video or Livestream to the Visu. Currently, most sources do not yet support this.                                              |
+---------------------------------------------------+---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| :doc:`Web <web/index>`                            | Adds an area to the Visu, where external websites can be displayed.                                                                   |
+---------------------------------------------------+---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| :doc:`WG_Plugin_Info <wgplugininfo/index>`        | Tbd.                                                                                                                                  |
+---------------------------------------------------+---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+

Widgets that can be included as plugin
======================================

These must be integrated in the ``meta`` area of ​​the :doc:`visu_config.xml <../xml-format>` before they can be used.

For example:

.. code-block:: xml

    <plugins>
        <plugin name="diagram" />
        <plugin name="colorchooser" />
        <plugin name="strftime" />
    </plugins>

+-----------------------------------------------------------------------+-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| Widget                                                                | Description                                                                                                           |
+=======================================================================+=======================================================================================================================+
| :doc:`ColorChooser <plugins/colorchooser/index>`                      | Color selection wheel for RGB illumination                                                                            |
+-----------------------------------------------------------------------+-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| :doc:`Diagram <plugins/diagram/index>`                                | RRD based graphs                                                                                                      |
+-----------------------------------------------------------------------+-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| :doc:`Diagram_Info <plugins/diagramm_info/index>`                     | Information display that opens a chart when you click                                                                 |
+-----------------------------------------------------------------------+-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| :doc:`PowerSpectrum <plugins/powerspectrum/index>`                    | Representation of the current spectrum                                                                                |
+-----------------------------------------------------------------------+-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| :doc:`Strftime <plugins/strftime/index>`                              | Display of the time in text form                                                                                      |
+-----------------------------------------------------------------------+-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| :doc:`RSSlog <plugins/rsslog/index>`                                  | RSS                                                                                                                   |
+-----------------------------------------------------------------------+-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| :doc:`RSS <plugins/rss/index>`                                        | RSS                                                                                                                   |
+-----------------------------------------------------------------------+-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| :doc:`Gauge <plugins/gauge/index>`                                    | pointer instrument                                                                                                    |
+-----------------------------------------------------------------------+-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| :doc:`CalendarList <plugins/calendarlist/index>`                      | External calendar                                                                                                     |
+-----------------------------------------------------------------------+-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| :doc:`Clock <plugins/clock/index>`                                    | Widget to display the time of a GA. (e.g. The WireGate)                                                               |
+-----------------------------------------------------------------------+-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| :doc:`SVG <plugins/svg/index>`                                        | Scalable vector graphics?                                                                                             |
+-----------------------------------------------------------------------+-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| :doc:`Timeout <plugins/timeout/index>`                                | Timeout                                                                                                               |
+-----------------------------------------------------------------------+-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| :doc:`UPNPController <plugins/upnpcontroller/index>`                  | UPNP Controller                                                                                                       |
+-----------------------------------------------------------------------+-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| :doc:`Speech <plugins/speech/index>`                                  | Uses the browsers builtin text-to-speech engine to speak text                                                         |
